DavidPH's Unending Mods

A series of mods meant to showcase the power of DS while leveraging that same
power to be simple, fun gameplay mods.


DavidPH's Unending Heresy (DUHeresy)

This one is for heretic, and focuses on making ammo optional, passive bonuses
from artifacts, and chickens.


DavidPH's Unending Magick (DUMagick)

A Hexen version of DUH with a spell-casting system.


DavidPH's Unending Damned (DUDamned)

Doom flavor, with a weapon/ability selection menu for maximum versatility.
